Love in the Unseen

“There is love in city lights, in the way a stranger smiles back, in the patience of a barista perfecting your coffee.”

Moments That Go Unnoticed

Every day, we witness small acts of love—on the subway, in a bookstore, in a handwritten note left on a café table. This space is dedicated to those fleeting, almost invisible gestures.
